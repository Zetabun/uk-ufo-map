Rendlesham Forest Incident

Dates: 26–28 Dec 1980 Location: Rendlesham Forest, Suffolk Near: RAF Woodbridge / RAF Bentwaters Highlight case
Image placeholder
Add a photo / illustration here when ready
Summary: Over several nights in late December 1980, U.S. Air Force personnel stationed at RAF Woodbridge/Bentwaters reported unusual lights and activity in and above Rendlesham Forest. The incident became known as “Britain’s Roswell” and is one of the most widely discussed UFO cases in the UK.

What was reported

The core reports describe bright, unexplained lights seen from the base and within the forest, including accounts of a glowing object and beams of light. The best‑known official document is a memo written by Deputy Base Commander Lt. Col. Charles I. Halt and sent to the UK Ministry of Defence on 13 January 1981.

Why it’s significant

Rendlesham is often cited because it involved multiple military witnesses and generated official paperwork, later becoming part of the UK’s wider body of UFO reporting and correspondence. The case remains disputed, with explanations ranging from misidentification and environmental factors to more extraordinary interpretations.
